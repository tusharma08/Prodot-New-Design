ABOUT US

What began as an iconoclast blue-themed brand with mere 1% market share in the late 1990s, today enjoys more than 70% monopoly in dot matrix printer cartridge. This has been the journey of ProDot, the first Indian brand to introduce the concept of dot matrix ribbon refills. ProDot made ribbon refills as easy and accessible as replacing ball point refills.

Datalink Industrial Corporation with its brand name ‘ProDot’ is an ISO 9001:2008; CE and FCC certified company and become a pioneer in the IT Industry today. With our own manufacturing house at Dehradun spread over 50,000 sq ft area, we offer in-house produced Computer Accessories and Peripherals in our ever-increasing network of 10000+ dealers selling more than 10 million products per annum. We have diversified our product portfolio to keyboard, mouse, antivirus products, surge protectors, refilling aids, laptop adapters, compatible laser cartridges, ribbons, etc.

We are truly a ‘Make in India’ company and are committed to the nation offering OEM quality products at 70% of the costs, being lucrative & profitable to both resellers & end user. Our products are available PAN India i.e we are proud to say that it available from Kashmir to Kanyakumari.

Timeline

1986

Started refilling DMP ribbon cartridges of end-users.

1989

Started making ‘DMP Ready Refills’, which was first ready to use DMP Refill of the country.

1991

First office at Pahar Ganj, Delhi same year ProDot was born.

2003

Setup a factory in SEZ at Himachal Pradesh and started in-house molding of DMP Ribbon shells.

2014

Setup a new factory to make keyboards & mouse, first in India.

2019

Entered security & surveillance industry and offered CCTV cameras and cables, wifi dongles etc.
